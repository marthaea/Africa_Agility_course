Oven-Baked Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (optional)
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

  4. Rub the chicken with olive oil and coat it evenly with the spice mixture.
  5. Place the chicken thighs on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or foil.
  6. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until the skin is crispy and the internal temperature reaches 165째F (75째C).
  7. Remove from the oven, squeeze fresh lemon juice over the chicken, and let it rest for 5 minutes.
  8. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve warm.
